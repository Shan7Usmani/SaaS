# Growth Strategy: PlacementOS

**Owner**: SaaS Growth Expert
**Goal**: First 100 paying customers with minimum budget

---

## 1. Acquisition Channels (Zero Budget)

### Campus Ambassador Program
- **Mechanic**: 1 student per college gets free Premium + referral link. They earn ₹100 per signup (cash or Amazon voucher).
- **Sourcing**: Target WhatsApp groups, Telegram groups ("CSE 2026 Batch", "XYZ College Placements"), LinkedIn ("Become Campus Lead — free Premium + stipend").
- **Cost**: ~₹10,000 for first 100 users. Wildly efficient CAC.

### College WhatsApp / Telegram Groups
- **Tactic**: Ambassadors share a "Free Placement Roadmap PDF" gated behind email signup. Daily drip-feed 1 tip for 7 days, then pitch Pro.
- **Peak timing**: July–Sept (placement season starts) and Jan–Feb (internship season).

### YouTube Shorts / Instagram Reels
- **Content hook**: "I solved 300 LeetCode questions in 30 days. Here's my tracker." Show the PlacementOS dashboard.
- **CTA**: "Link in bio for your free AI roadmap." Repurpose single 30-second clip across both platforms.

### LinkedIn Cold DM
- **Target**: Students posting "Solved 50 LeetCode today" or "Got my first OA."
- **Script**: *"Hey, saw you're grinding DSA. We built an AI that auto-syncs your LeetCode and tells you what to solve next. Free tier. Worth a try?"*

### College Fest / Placement-Season Events
- **Tactic**: Run a "Placement Readiness Challenge" — free 7-day cohort. Top 3 get free Premium. Collect emails → nurture to Pro.
- **Distribution**: Partner with college placement cells (they have mailing lists).

## 2. Referral System

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| Hook | "Invite a friend. You both get 7 days of Premium free." |
| Upgrade | After 7 days: "Your Premium expired. ₹499/mo, or invite 3 more friends for another 7 days." |
| Viral Loop | Student needs Premium for mock interview → invites 3 friends → each friend needs Premium → each invites 3 more |

**Double-sided incentive**: Referrer gets 7 days Premium. Referee gets 7 days Premium. "Refer 3 more" extends the cycle.

---

## 3. Viral Loop Design

```
Student signs up (free)
  → AI generates personalised roadmap
    → Student shares roadmap screenshot on LinkedIn/Twitter
      → "My Amazon SDE prep plan by PlacementOS 🔥"
        → Friend clicks, signs up, gets roadmap
          → Friend shares theirs → loop repeats
```

**Friction-removal tactics**:
- After roadmap generation, show: *"Share your roadmap & unlock 3 extra AI chats"*.
- One-click image download with watermark: "Preparing for Amazon SDE-1 @ PlacementOS".
- **Leaderboard loop**: "You ranked #42 in DSA this week. Share to claim your badge." Badge visible on profile → peer pressure → more shares.

## 5. Launch Plan — First 100 Paying Users

### Pre-launch (Weeks 1–2)
- Recruit 10 campus ambassadors from top 10 engineering colleges (IITs, NITs, IIITs, top private).
- Publish 5 SEO-optimised blog posts.
- Batch-record 20 Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts in one day.

### Launch Week (Week 3) — Free + Urgency
- **Day 1**: Post in 100 college WhatsApp/Telegram groups: *"PlacementOS — Free AI Roadmap. First 500 students get lifetime free basic plan."* (FOMO)
- **Day 2**: Ambassadors post their personalised roadmaps on LinkedIn with referral link.
- **Day 3**: Email blast to all signups: *"Your roadmap is ready. Upgrade to Pro for mock interviews — 50% off for first 100."*
- **Days 4–7**: Daily LinkedIn posts showing student progress screenshots (with permission).

### Post-launch (Weeks 4–8)
- Activate referral system.
- **Paid ads (only if needed)**: ₹500/day Meta ads targeting "engineering student India" with platform demo Reel. Kill if CAC > ₹200.
